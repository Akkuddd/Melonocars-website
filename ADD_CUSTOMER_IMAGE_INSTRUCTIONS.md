## To Add the Customer Image:

1. **Save the image** you provided (two men shaking hands in front of Toyota Innova car)
2. **Rename it to:** `happy-customer.jpg`
3. **Copy it to:** `public/uploads/happy-customer.jpg`

The image should be:
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Recommended size: 800x600px or similar aspect ratio
- The image path in the code is: `/uploads/happy-customer.jpg`

Once you add the image, it will automatically appear in the "Happy Customers" section on your homepage!
